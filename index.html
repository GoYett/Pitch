About Yett

Your gateway to citywide offline shopping

Yett connects shoppers with local retailers. Think Zomato Dining Out — but for fashion, lifestyle, and home décor. Yett mirror's the physical shopping landscape (malls, hubs, standalone stores) into structured categories that shoppers can explore with a single tap.

Our goal isn’t to pull people online ,but to add a digital layer to offline shopping, retaining its charm while enriching it.

What We’ve Built-Yett operates across three layers

Consumer App

  • Offline discovery & engagement
  • Explore malls, hubs & stores
  • Clean, category-first browsing

Merchant Dashboard

  • Brand storytelling & media
  • Offers, events & shopper analytics
  • Easy onboarding & updates

LLM-Optimized Backend (Yett 2.0)

  • AI-powered offline shopping chatbot
  • My Yett: curated journeys (wedding, home, deals, city)
  • Search & discoverability across AI platforms
